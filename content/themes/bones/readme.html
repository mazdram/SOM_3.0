A Lightweight Wordpress Theme

Small files, semantic, fast, full cross browser compatibility

Credit to Eddie Machado for building the fantastic Bones theme and Paul Irish & the HTML5 Boilerplate Yoast for some WP functions & optimization ideas. Bones is designed to make the life of developers easier. It’s built using HTML5 & has a strong semantic foundation. It’s constantly growing so be sure to check back often if you are a frequent user. I’m always open to contribution. :)
